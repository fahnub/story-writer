A RIPPLE SONG (Fables:-Ages 0 to 5)     Once a ripple came to land     In the golden sunset burning--     Lapped against a maiden's hand,     By the ford returning.     Dainty foot and gentle breast--     Here, across, be glad and rest.     "Maiden, wait," the ripple saith.     "Wait awhile, for I am Death!"
